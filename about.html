About Me

This page gives more background about who I am and how I am approaching this portfolio. While the homepage focuses more on the portfolio itself, this page focuses more on my academic path, my work style, and the direction I am trying to move toward.

Academic Path

I am currently a student at Texas Tech University majoring in Digital Media and Professional Communications. I chose this area because I am interested in digital creation, communication, and learning skills that can be useful in different career paths. CMPA 3301 has also given me a chance to practice coding, project planning, and organizing my work in a public GitHub repository.

How I Work

I know that I will most likely struggle due to my ADHD, especially when a project has several different parts to complete. Because of that, I try to keep the project realistic, follow the checklist, break the work into smaller steps, and check my progress as I go.

Personal Interests

Outside of school, I enjoy video games, reading, art, 3D modeling and printing, and random hands-on tinkering. Some of these interests are just for fun, but they also connect to the kind of creative and technical work I would like to keep improving.

Future Direction

I am still building experience, so this portfolio is not meant to present me as an established professional yet. Instead, it is a starting point that shows where I am now as a student and gives me something I can continue improving as I gain more experience.
